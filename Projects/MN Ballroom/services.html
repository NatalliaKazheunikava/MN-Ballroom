Introductory Package $75
The Introductory Special is a great way to start dancing, giving new students a taste of all that we have to offer. It includes one group dance class and two 30-minute private lessons (per person or per couple). Sign up now to start your dancing journey!

Wedding Consultation $40
Create an unforgettable first dance with our expert instructors. We recommend starting with our wedding consultation, where we can get to know your goals, form a plan, and begin moving forward.

Group Class $18
Group classes can be a great place to begin and experiment with different dance styles. It is also a cost effective way to learn many dances. Partners are not necessary, since we frequently rotate throughout the class. However, if you wish to not rotate partners, just let your instructor know.

Private Lesson $90
Private lessons are the best and fastest way to learn Ballroom and Latin dances. Sessions are one-on-one with our expert dance instructors. Private lessons are available for singles and couples of all ages and levels. During the 45 min lesson, we will focus on your specific needs and goals.
